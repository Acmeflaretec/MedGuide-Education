Kazakh National Medical University, Kazakhstan

In 1930 by RSFSR SP council decision the first institute in Kazakhstan begun. The first rector was S.D. Asfendiyarov, a graduating student of Leningrad Navy Medical Academy. For great services and peoples general population health development the medical institute was awarded the Order of the Crimson Banner of Labour in April of 1981.

In 2001 Kazakh State medical university given its name S.D. Asfendiyarov was awarded the high nationwide status of the school. KNMU is 85 Years of age Govt. University. KNMU ranked 1st best medical institute in all of Kazakhstan. KNMU is in ALMATY. Proper Native american Hostel & Indian Food(Veg & Non Veg)Indian Warden Separately for Boys & Girls Hostel. NMC Test out (FMGE) preparation from the first year. More than fivehundred students are studying. Function training is in British medium. Its only 1626 km from New Delhi. Currency of Kazakstan is Tenge.

Kazakh National Medical University is an innovation-oriented and sociallyresponsible University, a leader in Central South America in preparation of competitive multi level health professionals and pharmacies through the execution of the competency based model of as well as pharmaceutical education, with intensive involvement of local and foreign scientists and consistently boosting the scientific probable.

Admission Criteria

Comparing from India for admission in MBBS, it is a 4.5 years course, here Kazakhstan it is of 5 years. Apart from this difference in duration the pattern is somewhat similar to Indian pattern. After completion applicant will be awarded a MBBS degree which is recognised globally.

Students are required to submit their educational documents needed in academic formalities in university while admission procedure. Once a student receives the admission letter and gets visa confirmed, he is set to go for MBBS studies.

Here MedGuide Education plays main role in helping you. We take all your responsibilities related to your Admission in university.

An aspirant carrying a minimum of 50% marks in 12th Standard in Physics, Chemistry and Biology and the NEET Qualified according to NMC guidelines.

There is no entrance test required, admission is granted on first come first served basis.

The student should have completed the age of 17 years on or before 31st December of the year of admission.

University Affiliation

Education system of Kazakhstan is beyond par excellence with best infrastructure & amenities. All the government Universities are recognized by many International councils like NMC, WHO, UNESCO, SCO etc. Degree from Kazakhstan medical universities are accepted worldwide.

As Kazakhstan has 5 year MBBS program, hence most of the students who wish to complete MBBS in 5 years, prefer to go to Kazakhstan. Fees and living cost is very low and almost same as other central asian countries like Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, China and even Russia.
