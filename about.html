About us

At MedGuide Education, we are committed to transforming dreams into reality by empowering students and guiding them towards academic success. Our team of experienced consultants provides tailored solutions to help students achieve their educational goals. We specialize in guiding students through the complexities of academia, providing educational opportunities, and enhancing their learning journey

We offer educational support and guidance in various countries such as:
  • Russia
  • Kazakhstan
  • Uzbekistan
  • Georgia
  • Kyrgyzstan

Vision & Mission:

Vision: Our vision is to give feathers to your dreams by providing free counseling, guidance, and support for students pursuing MBBS abroad. We aim to have a friendly approach to turning your dreams into reality.

Mission: Our mission is to offer the best educational guidance to students seeking to study MBBS abroad at top universities that are recognized by Indian and international accreditation bodies at affordable costs.

Supporting System

Guiding students towards successful MBBS programs abroad with personalized counseling and comprehensive assistance.

Professional Counselling

Offering professional guidance tailored to career choices and future prospects for MBBS abroad

Information Provision

Complete Assistance

Application Process

Organizing all application requirements for MBBS programs in Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, and more.
