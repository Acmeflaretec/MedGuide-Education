Astana Medical University, Kazakhstan

Astana Medical University is one of the biggest Medical Universities in Kazakhstan. Astana Medicinal University was established in year 1964. AMU is a dynamically developing educational, research, clinical and cultural center,developing pertnership with educational, research and clinical organizations, providing a high level of traning of qualified personnel and providing medical services, corresponding to the established requirements and advanced international experience. For many years the University has been ranked as one of the leading medical Universities in Kazakhstan. US, Singapore, Europe, and Russia are our academic partners. Success of our graduate students is in finding the employment and becoming a great doctor is an example of the quality of education we are offering.

Admission Criteria

The candidate must have passed 10+2 with physics, chemistry Biology and English from an recognized educational establishment with min marks of 50% in physics, Chemistry & Biology and English can apply for MD/MBBS program. Students should be of Minimum 17 Years old while applying for the course during the same year, here 2020.

Eligibility Criteria

•You are at least 17 and maximum 25 years old on or before 31st December of the admission year.

•You have passed Class 12th with PCB and English subjects from a board recognized by the Authorities in India.

•You have secured a minimum of 50% in PCB in 10+2.

•You have qualified NEET Exam.

Admission Process

Process for getting admission in MBBS in KAZAKHSTAN is very simple. Kazakhstan Medical Universities do not require Indian students to pass any entrance exams for admission in MBBS Course. Admissions are based on a first come first served, subject to the candidate satisfying the eligibility criteria set by the Kazakhstan medical University and NMC (*for Indian students).
