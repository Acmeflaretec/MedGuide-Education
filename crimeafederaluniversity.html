Crimea Federal University, Russia

About University

Crimea Federal University is one of the top Universities to pursue MBBS from Russia. The faculty of General Medicine is one of the top priorities of Indian students to study Medicine. The University is located in Simferopol (Crimea, Russia). This educational institution is considered to be one of the most prominent Medical Schools in Russia.

In the year 1918, the University was known as the Medical Faculty of Taurida University. However, in 1931, it became an autonomous unit called Crimea Medical Institute. Since 1995, this University has been named in honor of Sergey IvanovichGeogievsky (rector of the University from 1951 to 1970). Later, in 1998, this higher school became one of the top Universities with the 4th highest accreditation level.

Benefits of Medical Education in Russia (MBBS in Russia) for Indian students

1. Comprehensive Education

Russia has always been known for training physicians and specialists in natural sciences. The Russian Medical Universities have always secured a place among Top Medical Universities. Russia is the part of the European Credit Transfer System which makes various Universities and colleges of the rest of Europe easily accessible to the graduates of all the Universities of the Russian Federation.

2. Excellent Educational Standards

Russian Medical Universities boast great infrastructure, laboratories, a large number of hospitals and clinics linked with them in the city, and association with other medical institutions of the country as well as other European and Asian Countries. So, doing MBBS from Russia is at par with world standards.

3. Low Tuition Fee

Most Russian Medical Universities are Government Universities, where the cost of education is subsidized by the government which in turn makes the Tuition Fee significantly low as compared to other countries. This makes Studying MBBS in Russia very affordable along with good quality.

4. A chance to learn the local language and dialect

Learning a new language opens new doors and new ways in which a person interacts and thinks about new problems, it has been proven scientifically that a person who speaks more than one language is smarter than their peers. So, moving abroad and getting along with locals will help students learn the local dialect and grow. The Russian Medical Universities also offer special language courses to interested students who wish to study language as a separate course.

5. The medium of instruction is English

Being able to learn a new language is an Advantage, nevertheless, the students who join (MBBS in Russia) are taught in the English Language for the complete duration at Crimea State Medical University.

6. Well-Equipped Dormitories and Campuses

The Crimea State Medical University has hospitals and clinics linked to it along with various departments spread out throughout the city. All the departments are well equipped with all the necessary instruments and apparatuses. The Dormitories are also well equipped with all the necessary facilities such as 24hrs running hot and cold water.

7. Easy adaption for International Students

Since there are a lot of International Students in the University from various countries pursuing various courses in pharmacy, dentistry as well as MBBS from Russia, it is quite easy for new students to adapt and assimilate into new places and culture with the help of a friendly and caring University staff.

8. Exposure to New Culture

Russia and India have always had a special relationship with each other. Both countries have always embraced each other’s culture with great enthusiasm. So, doing MBBS from Russia will give students exposure to a new and welcoming culture which will help them grow overall.

9. Sports

The Russian universities provide sports facilities to the students on the university campus and the hostel campus as well. Students always have the opportunity to get involved in sports activities. Students who are studying MBBS in Russia even get a chance to play on the national and international level on behalf of the University.

10. Exciting and New Life!

Student life is the time in an Individual’s life during which one grows at the maximum rate, be it in a physical sense, or mental sense. The pattern of Education in Russia is no doubt a little different as compared to India, nevertheless, the Educational quality is at par with International and European Standards.
