About Tbilisi City

Information about Tbilisi city mentioned below:

Tbilisi is the capital city of Georgia, An old town with a large history of periods under Persian and Russian rule.

Tbilisi is also a part of the UNESCO world heritage list. Known for architecture comprises of Eastern Orthodox churches, ornate art nouveau buildings, and Soviet Modernist structures. Looming over it all are Narikala, a reconstructed 4th-century fortress, and Kartlis Deda, an iconic statue of the “Mother of Georgia.

About Caucasus International University, Georgia

Caucasus International University was founded as a medical institute in 1995 and is located in Tbilisi, Georgia. The university attracts more foreign university entrants and students for doing MBBS.

Caucasus International University has numerous and highly qualified alumni. Currently, more than 700 medical students are coming from various countries and the number of students is increasing per term.

Caucasus International University creates a modern learning and teaching atmosphere by implementing the innovative methodology in the education system, as well as through intercultural education and diverse university life.

The university ensures the upbringing of a generation with democratic values that will be competitive at the local and international labor markets.

The students looking to seek admission in Caucasus International University must fill the application form available on the official website till July 15.

Eligibility Criteria

If the students strive to get admission in the medicine program at Caucasus International University, then the following requirements mentioned below have to be met:

• The applicant's age should be 17 years old as on 31st December of the admission year.

• The applicant must obtain at least 50% marks in the higher secondary examination or equivalent examination from the recognized board.

• The candidates must qualify NEET examination to get MBBS admission at Caucasus International University.

• The general category candidates require 50% scores, and SC/ST/OBC category candidates require 45% scores.

Documents Required

The students must bring the important documents required during the MBBS admission process at Caucasus International University:

• The candidate must submit the application form.

• Candidates must have NEET examination results.

• The academic mark sheet and passing certificate (original and copy).

• The candidate must carry a transfer certificate from the previous institution.

• A valid passport is essential.

• The birth certificate must be submitted during admission.
